ALI HUSSAIN

Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam 15 october 1931-27 july 2015) was an India

aerospace scientist who served as the 11th president of india from 2002 to 2007. he was born and

raised an Rameswarm, Tmail Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering. he spent the

next four decades as a scientist and science administrator, mainly at the Defence Research and

Development Organisation (DRDO ) and india space Resarch Organisation(ISRO) and was

intimately involved in India Civilian space program and military missible development efforts.

[1] he thus came to be known as the Missile Man of india for his work on the development of

ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.[2][3][4] He also played a pivotal organisational.

technical, and political role in india pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998, the first since the original

nuclear test by india in 1974
